About
Contextual understanding

Read deeper.
Understand everything.

Lexio gives you the precise meaning of any word in context — not a dictionary definition, but what it actually means in the sentence you're reading.

How it works

1

Paste any text

Drop in a paragraph from an article, book, email, or anything else — any language works.

2

Analyze it

Lexio reads the full context and prepares every word for instant lookup. Takes about a second.

3

Click any word

Tap a word to see its exact meaning in that sentence, where it comes from, and how to say it.

Everything you need to understand

Contextual definitions

Not what the word means in general — what it means right here, in this sentence, the way this author used it.

12 languages

Paste text in Spanish, French, German, Russian, Japanese, and more. Get definitions in whatever language you prefer.

IPA pronunciation

See the phonetic transcription of every word and hear it spoken aloud with a single tap.

Etymology

Understand where words come from. Knowing a word's roots makes it stick — and makes the next related word easier.

Word bank

Save words you want to remember. Your personal vocabulary list, always one click away.
