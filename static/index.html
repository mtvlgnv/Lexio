Features

Contextual definitions

Not what the word means in general — what it means right here, in this sentence, the way this author used it.

11 languages

Paste text in Spanish, French, German, Russian, Japanese, and more. Get definitions in whatever language you prefer.

IPA pronunciation

See the phonetic transcription of every word and hear it spoken aloud with a single tap.

Etymology

Understand where words come from. Knowing a word's roots makes it stick — and makes the next related word easier.

Word bank

Save words you want to remember. Your personal vocabulary list, always one click away.

3 reading modes

Switch between Fast, Balanced, and Deep — powered by OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic — each tuned to a different depth of analysis.
