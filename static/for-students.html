Handling complex texts

Because spleen in Baudelaire is not spleen in Biology.

A regular dictionary gives you a list of possible meanings. Lexio uses Claude Sonnet 4.5 and Gemini 2.5 Flash to give you the right meaning for the specific text you're assigned.

Deep mode for philosophy

Kant's noumenon or Heidegger's Dasein? Lexio's Deep mode (Claude Sonnet 4.5) reads the surrounding paragraph to tell you which specific sense the author is invoking in their philosophical system. No more guessing.

Perfect for continental philosophy and theory.

Archaic words & literature

When Shakespeare says discover, he doesn't mean "find"—he means "reveal". When reading the classics, the Victorian novel, or the King James Bible, Lexio knows the period-specific senses of words.

Stop looking up words that look familiar but feel wrong.

Terms of art

Academic papers are full of jargon and specific terms of art. Lexio recognizes when a common word is being used in a highly specific, disciplinary way and defines it accordingly.

Read everywhere

Works on printed books and locked PDFs.

University courses love to assign scanned book chapters as locked PDFs where you can't highlight or copy the text. Lexio instantly extracts the text and lets you tap any word for a contextual definition. The book stays a book, and you get your definitions.
