Input Monitoring permission
A second, separate permission the double-tap trigger also needs — macOS doesn't let apps check or request this one, so if double-tap does nothing at all (not even opening Siri), turn it on manually.
Screen Recording permission
Lets Lexio read text near your cursor via OCR when Accessibility can't — chat messages, subtitles, images, locked PDFs. Turn this on to get contextual definitions everywhere, not just in browsers and documents.
